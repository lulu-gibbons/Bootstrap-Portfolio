ABOUT ME

Hello, I’m Lacey.

I’m a Seattle-based web designer and developer who grew up on a ranch in Montana. My educational background is in fine art and cartography and while I spend my visits home branding, calving and harvesting, when I'm in Seattle I prefer to let my creative side fill my days.

I’ve been working on websites for several years as a web administrator, e-commerce specialist, business owner and hobbyist. When I’m not creating through code, I’m taking pictures, loving my dog and traveling the world with my hubby.
